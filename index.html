About Us

Who We Are

Glitter Bliss Vestiti is a Nigerian-owned lifestyle store bringing you two things we love: beautiful fragrances and quality clothing. We are proudly based in Nigeria and serve customers across the country.

Our catalogue is split into two collections. Our Fragrance Collection features carefully selected eau de parfums for every occasion. Our Fashion Collection covers wear for men, women and children — from everyday casual pieces to statement outfits for special occasions.

We keep things simple: there's no account to create and no sign-up required. Browse the store, tap on anything you like to see full details, and reach out to us directly on WhatsApp to place your order. We reply personally to every message and can help with sizing, availability and delivery across Nigeria.
