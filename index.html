Each bottle of Aurelia is hand-assembled in our historic Grasse atelier. Our master perfumers employ centuries-old distillation techniques to extract the purest essences from Damask roses harvested at dawn.

The heavy glass flacon, adorned with a 24k gold-plated cap, reflects the uncompromising craftsmanship that defines the Glitter Bliss Vestiti legacy. This is more than a scent; it is a wearable piece of art.
